A Home Away From Home (HAH) was created to fill a critical gap in foster care — providing short-term respite care in a safe, stable, home-like environment while equipping youth with the tools they need to build a better future.

My Story

My Experience

I've dedicated my career to child welfare, licensing foster homes and working alongside families, social workers, and community partners across North Carolina.

The Challenge

Too many times, I saw children waiting for placement with nowhere safe, stable, or supportive to go — especially when they needed it most.

The Pivot

I knew I couldn't just hope for change — I had to be part of the solution. I pivoted from the limitations I saw and created what was missing.

The Mission

I created the home I needed to see — one filled with stability, dignity, and opportunity for every child who walks through our doors.

“Every child deserves more than a placement. They deserve a place that feels like home.”
— Alexis Williams, Founder
