What We Do

Care that meets the moment.

From emergency respite stays to life-skills support, everything we do is designed around one goal: giving children in foster care a safe, steady place to land.

Our Services

What we provide

Short-Term Respite Care

Temporary stays — from overnight to several weeks — while agencies secure the right long-term placement.

A Home-Like Environment

Private bedrooms, shared family meals, and predictable routines in a licensed, supervised home setting.

Life Skills & Enrichment

Homework help, daily responsibilities, and age-appropriate life-skills coaching that follows each child forward.

Agency Coordination

Close communication with caseworkers, DSS offices, and managed-care partners from intake through transition.

Foster Family Relief

Planned respite that gives foster parents time to rest and recharge — helping placements last longer.

Trauma-Informed Care

Every interaction is grounded in safety, trust, and respect — because how a child is cared for matters as much as where.

Who We Serve

Built for the whole circle of care.

HAH supports everyone working to keep a child safe — not just the child in our care.

  • Children & youth in foster care awaiting or between placements
  • Foster families who need planned or emergency relief
  • Social workers & case managers seeking a safe, immediate option
  • County DSS offices & agencies across North Carolina

Inside the Home

What a stay looks like

A Warm Arrival

Every child is welcomed with a prepared room, fresh essentials, and a calm introduction to the home.

Daily Rhythm

Regular meals, school support, chores, and downtime — the predictable structure kids thrive on.

Ongoing Support

Caring adults check in daily, and we keep caseworkers updated on how each child is doing.

A Good Goodbye

Transitions are planned, not rushed — children leave with their belongings, records, and confidence.
