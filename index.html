Why HAH Exists

Safety First

Children waiting on placement deserve more than a stopgap. Our home is licensed, supervised, and built around trauma-informed care.

Home, Not a Facility

Real bedrooms, shared meals, and daily routine. A place that looks and feels like home — because that's what it is.

Tools for the Future

Life skills, structure, and encouragement — so every stay, however short, leaves a child better equipped for what's next.

Short-Term Respite Care

A bridge when it matters most.

Respite care offers children a safe, temporary home while caseworkers secure the right long-term placement — and gives foster families the support they need to keep going.

  • Immediate, short-term placement in a licensed home
  • Stability and routine during a stressful transition
  • Support for foster families and social workers
  • Coordination with agencies across North Carolina
